How It Works

1

History

Interview the AI patient to gather their medical history and symptoms.

2

Exam

Perform a physical examination by reviewing different body systems.

3

Tests

Order diagnostic tests to gather more information about the condition.

4

Formulate a differential diagnosis and select your final diagnosis.

5

Debrief

Receive detailed feedback on your clinical reasoning and decision-making.

Who It's For

This simulator is designed for high school students and pre-medical students who want to:

Explore Medicine

Get hands-on experience with clinical reasoning and patient interaction in a safe, fictional environment.

Practice Skills

Develop your ability to ask the right questions, interpret findings, and make diagnostic decisions.

What You'll Practice

💬

Communication

Learn to ask effective questions and build rapport with patients.

🧠

Clinical Reasoning

Develop your ability to think through complex medical cases.

📊

Decision-Making

Practice choosing appropriate tests and making diagnoses.
